Why we give

Canopy Roads was planted in 1996 with a single vision: to help unchurched people become fully devoted followers of Christ. For nearly 30 years your generosity has funded Sunday services, children's ministry, student programs, community outreach, and global missions.

We believe giving is an act of worship — a tangible declaration that God is first. We steward every dollar with accountability, intentionality, and gratitude. Questions about how we use funds? Contact us at alicia@canopyroads.org.
