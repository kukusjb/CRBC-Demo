What happens when we arrive?

1
Check in at the Kidz desk
Our volunteers meet you at check-in and get your child registered quickly — no waiting around.
2
You get a matching security tag
Your child receives a tag linked to a code only you hold. No one else can pick them up.
3
Head to the service — they're in great hands
Screened volunteers, monitored facilities, and security staff give you a genuinely worry-free hour.
4
Pick up with your tag after service
Show your coded sticker at pick-up and your child is released to you — and only you.

What your child will experience

More than just childcare

Every Sunday in Canopy Kidz Preschool is designed around one goal: helping your little one take their very first steps toward a real relationship with God.

🎵

Worship & Bible Teaching

Through engaging, age-appropriate teaching, songs, and hands-on activities, we guide little ones toward what it means to know God personally. Every lesson points to Jesus in a way a preschooler can actually grasp and hold onto.

👫

Small Group Classes

Close-knit groups by age means your child is always with kids at their same developmental stage — so the teaching connects and the friendships are real. Consistent volunteers build genuine relationships week over week.

🔐

Your Child's Safety

Safety is the foundation everything else is built on. Our check-in system, background-screened volunteers, and security-monitored facility all work together so you can be fully present in the worship service — not worried about your child.

📱

Parent Partnership

What happens in Canopy Kidz on Sunday is meant to continue at home all week. The CRBC App includes a dedicated Kids Tile with parent resources, take-home materials, and ways to keep the conversation going with your child.

Curriculum

Built on the four Big Truths

We use Puggles®, a program from AWANA designed specifically to introduce early preschoolers to what they need to know most about God and His love.

Puggles focuses on the four Big Truths of the Bible that form a strong, lasting first impression of God — who He is, how much He loves us, and how we can respond to that love. These aren't just Sunday school facts; they're the foundation your child will build their whole life on.

Learn more about Puggles at awana.org →
🐣

Puggles® by AWANA

For early preschoolers (birth–age 2). Four Big Truths communicated through songs, stories, and simple activities designed for the youngest learners.

  • 1 God made everything — including you
  • 2 God loves us very much
  • 3 Sin keeps us from God
  • 4 Jesus is God's Son — our Savior
