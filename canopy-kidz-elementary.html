What your child will experience

Built for elementary-age kids

Sunday mornings in Canopy Kidz Elementary are designed around how kids this age actually learn — through music, story, activity, and real relationships with consistent adults who know their names.

🎵

Large Group Worship & Teaching

Every Sunday kicks off with engaging music, games, and a Bible lesson taught in a way that lands with elementary kids. This isn't a lecture — it's an experience. Kids walk out knowing what the story was about and why it matters to them.

Watch recent lessons online →
👥

Small Groups by Grade

After large group, kids move into smaller, grade-level classes where they dig into the lesson, ask questions, memorize Scripture, and build genuine friendships. The same volunteers show up week after week — relationships are the point.

🛡

Safety You Can Count On

Background-screened volunteers, a security-monitored facility, and a coded check-in system work together so you can fully engage in the worship service without a second thought about your child's wellbeing.

📱

Tools for Home

Faith doesn't stop at the church door. The CRBC App includes a dedicated Kids Tile with take-home content, parent discussion guides, and resources to help you continue Sunday's lesson throughout the week.

Download the CRBC App →

Curriculum

The Gospel Project for Kids

Every Sunday morning, kids in Canopy Kidz experience the big story of the Bible through The Gospel Project for Kids — a Christ-centered curriculum that reveals God's plan to rescue His people through His Son, Jesus Christ.

Kids are immersed in the gospel through every story, theological concept, and call to mission from Genesis to Revelation. The Bible isn't taught as a collection of moral lessons — it's taught as one unified story of redemption, and Jesus is the center of every single lesson.

Heart transformation can only take place when a child truly encounters the gospel. That's what this curriculum is designed to do, week after week, year after year.

Beyond Sunday mornings

More ways to get involved

Canopy Kidz Elementary extends beyond Sunday services — we take the mission to local schools and go all-in on summer with VBS.

🏫 Good News Club® Weekly
Tuesdays 2:50–4:30pm Killearn Lakes Elementary

Sponsored by CEF (Child Evangelism Fellowship®), Good News Club is a weekly after-school Bible club for K–5th graders in the community. Kids experience dynamic Bible lessons, engaging songs, inspiring missionary stories, Scripture memory, special prizes, and snacks. It's a missions trip every single week — to a school right down the road. Good News Club resumes in the fall.

Contact Jenna to volunteer Learn more about Good News Club → Watch the story →
🍀 VBS 2026 — Emerald Crossing Summer
Summer 2026 CRBC Campus

In a world filled with worry and confusion, kids need to know there is a God who cares for them. This summer, lads and lasses will journey through Ireland's lush landscapes at Emerald Crossing — exploring Psalm 23 and the life of shepherd-king David. Kids will discover how to cross from worry to peace, from fear to faith, and from uncertainty to a firm foundation in God's Word through a relationship with the Good Shepherd.
