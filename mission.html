Our mission & vision

In the next twenty years, we will raise up
a new generation of Christ followers by becoming
an outwardly-focused church of neighborhood
and marketplace missionaries who live S.E.N.T.

We believe the most strategic way to change our community's future is to transform families and the next generation — starting right where we already are.

What is S.E.N.T.? Mission Opportunities

Living sent

We can change the world when we partner with God

"Living S.E.N.T." is a missional framework where each letter stands for a specific everyday practice. These habits are simple, scalable, and reproducible — and when practiced with gospel intentionality, they activate every person to live as an ordinary, everyday missionary right where they already are.

You don't need to travel overseas or have a seminary degree. The mission field is your neighborhood, your workplace, your kids' school. S.E.N.T. gives you a way to be intentional about it.

Get trained in Living S.E.N.T. → email Justin
S
See People Like Jesus

Noticing the people already around you and choosing to see them the way Jesus does — with compassion, curiosity, and genuine care. Pay attention to who God has placed in your path.

E
Eat with People Like Jesus

Sharing meals across difference. The table is one of the most powerful places for relationship — and relationship is how the gospel travels most naturally.

N
Neighbor People Like Jesus

Becoming a genuinely good neighbor — to the people on your street, in your apartment building, or sitting beside you at school or work. Presence precedes influence.

T
Talk About Jesus

Having natural, honest conversations about faith — not as a rehearsed script, but out of the overflow of what God has done in your own life. You have a story worth telling.

Who we are

Our values are C.L.E.A.R.

Five commitments that shape how we worship, serve, and love one another — and how we engage our city.

C
Community

We build and preserve relationships, focus on families, and care for one another — because the church is a body, not an audience.

L
Life Transformation

We cultivate Christ-like character and pursue a Christ-centered life — because following Jesus changes who we are from the inside out.

E
Evangelism

We engage our community, befriend the lost, and share the gospel — because every person around us matters to God.

A
Authenticity

We live with transparency and integrity — because a church that pretends has nothing to offer a world that already knows it's broken.

R
Relevance

We apply Scripture to everyday life, make truth understandable to everyone, and engage cultural issues honestly — because the gospel speaks to real life.

Mission Opportunities

Whether it's local or international, Canopy Roads values the command Jesus gives us to go and make disciples. We are always seeking opportunities to be mission-oriented — and we'd love for you to be part of our journey.

🚨

Disaster & Emergency Relief Team (D.E.R.T.)

The purpose of DERT is to share Christ in times of crisis — whether a natural disaster, a fallen tree, or acts of vandalism. This team shows up to help, no strings attached, as an expression of the gospel in action.

International Mission Trips

Canopy Roads desires to take the gospel to every corner of the earth. We partner with Hands for Hope for international trips — with a focus on Nicaragua — to serve communities and share the love of Christ across borders.

Serve in Good News Club

Go on a mission trip every week — at a local public school. Good News Club is a weekly after-school Bible club for elementary-age kids, with dynamic lessons, songs, missionary stories, and Scripture memory. Your presence changes a child's life.
