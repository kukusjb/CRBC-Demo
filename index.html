Welcome

You're welcome here, exactly as you are.

Whether you're walking through something hard, searching for the first time, or looking for a community that goes deeper — there's a place for you at Canopy Roads. We're not perfect people. We're forgiven people, grateful to be on this journey together.

Our church has been rooted in Northwest Tallahassee for decades. We're raising families, walking with neighbors, and trying to live out the gospel in practical ways — in our schools, our workplaces, and around our tables.

Our mission

Raising up the next generation of Christ followers

We believe the most strategic way to change our community's future is to transform families and the next generation — by living S.E.N.T.

S
Seeing people like Jesus

Noticing people around us and choosing to see them the way Jesus does — with compassion, curiosity, and care.

E
Eating with people like Jesus

Sharing meals across difference. The table is where relationships are built and the gospel travels.

N
Neighboring people

Becoming genuinely good neighbors — to the people on our street, in our apartment complex, or beside us at school.

T
Talking about Jesus

Having natural, honest conversations about faith — not as a script, but out of the overflow of what God has done in our lives.
