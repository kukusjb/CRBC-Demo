Small Groups

You're not meant to
do this alone.

Fully devoted followers of Christ connect with believers weekly. Joining a community matures our faith, creates a sense of belonging, and gives us examples to follow.

Why join a group

1

Faith grows in community

Weekly connection with other believers keeps faith from becoming a solo habit. Iron sharpens iron.

2

You'll have people who know you

A group is the difference between attending a church and belonging to one. It's where people notice when you're missing.

3

Real-life discipleship happens here

Groups aren't just Bible studies — they're where you learn to apply Scripture in your actual life, from people in the thick of it too.

Not sure which group? Email us and we'll help you find one.

Point-to-Point Men's Ministry

A place for men to learn, help, and hold each other accountable

Point-to-Point is focused on providing a place for men to learn from one another, help one another, and drive one another to be focused on our walk in faith. Life's not perfect — things are tough and obstacles are everywhere. But if we focus on our final destination in Christ, we can go over, under, and through those obstacles together.

Every Monday 7:00pm Lofty Pursuit, 6267 Old Water Oak Rd
